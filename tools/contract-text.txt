INFINITY POOLS
PROJECT
OVERVIEW
The project scope includes pre-construction design coordination followed by the construction of a
custom 15' x 25' geometric in-ground pool with an attached raised spa. The pool will feature a 5' x
15' sun shelf (12-inch depth) with integrated descending entry steps, as well as a raised 7.5' x 7.5' spa
equipped with six integrated jets, LED lighting, and a 3-foot-wide cascading spillover.
Construction will consist of engineered rebar, 10–12" shotcrete walls, and all required plumbing and
electrical systems installed in accordance with applicable codes. Interior finishes will include a
PebbleTec surface, waterline tile, and coping selections, all to be finalized during the design phase.
The scope also includes the installation of all pool equipment and the equipment pad, including
pump, filtration system, salt cell, plumbing for blower, 400K BTU heater, and LED lighting, along with
final system activation and site cleanup.
